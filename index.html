School Vision and Mission

Vision

"Al Dhafra Private Academy will enshrine educational excellence, where culture and tradition are honored and everyone is safe. Students attending Al Dhafra Private Academy will become internationally-minded, global citizens, possessing the skills and character to thrive in a challenging and changing world."

Mission

"Empowering students to become responsible global citizens, we cultivate a nurturing environment that fosters well-being, inclusion, and sustainability. Rooted in the rich tapestry of UAE national identity, we set high expectation for academic excellence and inspire lifelong learning."
